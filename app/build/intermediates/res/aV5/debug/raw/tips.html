How i can improve the text recognition:

  • Good lightning is most important. Use the flashlight if possible.
  • Blurry text is hard to read for me. Make sure that the camera has properly focussed the text
  • Hold the phone parallel to the text when taking the photo
  • Experiment with different iso values. High iso values can lead to image noise that makes it harder to recognize the letters
  • Most of the time 5 to 7 megapixels is enough resolution to get good results. Higher values will often only increase the processing time.

"Additional tips"

  • You can speed up the text recognition by cropping the taken photo
  • I'm best at recognizing Text from books, magazines or new papers. Ornate or colorful letters are problematic for me.
  • I'm having equally difficulties when reading light text from dark backgrounds.
